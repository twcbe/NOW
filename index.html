NETWORK OF WOMEN


An initiative by Thoughtworks N.O.W. (Network of Women), is a forum for curious women from all walks of life, looking to be part of an open and inclusive network, to come together to learn from each other and be inspired. It provides a positive and motivating environment by bringing fresh content, wonderful speakers and trainers, and innovative formats.

Did you know the five largest tech companies on the planet (Amazon, Apple, Facebook, Google and Microsoft) only have a workforce of about 34.4% women?


We are all aware about the Gender gap that exists in women in Tech and STEM in general. Women and Technology is a sweet spot where the gap still applies. Even though organisations from their part take measures on hiring, supporting and encouraging women folks, it is still a challenge for many Women to climb ladders in the organisation. There are external factors like society, family, culture, politics and so on that comes into the play posing impediments in our journey of leadership.


Come join us in listening to the experiences of 3 Key speakers from ThoughtWorks, with one commonality. Leading all the way with more than 10 years of experience.
