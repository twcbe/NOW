NETWORK OF WOMEN


An initiative by Thoughtworks N.O.W. (Network of Women), is a forum for curious women from all walks of life, looking to be part of an open and inclusive network, to come together to learn from each other and be inspired. It provides a positive and motivating environment by bringing fresh content, wonderful speakers and trainers, and innovative formats.


For any further questions / suggestions write to us at:
women-coimbatore@thoughtworks.com

Do you feel a constant tug-of-war between
the competing demands of work and home?

If so, you’re not alone, welcome to the club!



Especially with COVID the boundary between Home & Work has blurred taking a toll on our mental & physical health. Lots of Organisations have brought in programmes to ease out this challenge.


Here is a forum to converse about work-life balance, it's necessity in our lives and most importantly sharing of experiences, learnings & tips & tricks to help you be better at it. We also have an exciting activity to stir up your thoughts! So don’t miss it.

Speakers

Sujitha Selvaraj

Sujitha Selvaraj has about 9 years of experience in the IT industry with a degree in engineering and masters in business administration. She joined ThoughtWorks in 2015 as a Business Analyst. She has then played various roles such as program BA, Product Owner, etc., She currently plays the role of Office Principal for ThoughtWorks Coimbatore. She likes to spend her leisure time in travel, fitness and gardening.

Banu Pavithra

Banu is a Project Manager in ThoughtWorks with over 15 years of experience in the IT Industry. Besides work, Banu enjoys parenting her 7 year old son and keeps herself excited with her hobbies of doing rangolis and garlanding.

Siddarth Kengadaran

Siddarth self taught designer with 7+ Years of experience, I don't see work and personal life different but both as part of my life. Trying to be an avid reader preferably non-fiction. Fav books on work life balance are ‘Indistractable’ & ‘Make Time’.

Akilaa S

Akila is QA at TW and has been with TW for close to 2 years now. She is currently living at Coimbatore and after work hours you can find her involved in her art works.

Rathinakumar Ponnusamy

Rathinakumar is lead consultant at ThoughtWorks where he plays the role of a data and analytics subject matter expert.
